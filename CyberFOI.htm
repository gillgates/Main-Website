Cybersecurity & Freedom of Information

In a world where nearly every aspect of society depends on digital systems, cybersecurity has become one of the most urgent and complicated challenges of the modern era...

Over the past decade, cyber-attacks have evolved rapidly...

Governments and organizations worldwide have therefore made cybersecurity a national priority...

At the same time, democratic societies depend on transparency to function properly...

FOI reinforces trust between citizens and their institutions...

However, as cybersecurity strengthens, the amount of information considered “classified” or “restricted” can grow as well...

On the other hand, poorly managed FOI systems can unintentionally put people at risk...

Finding the right balance is one of the greatest challenges modern governments face...

Technology can help solve tensions between cybersecurity and FOI...

Real-world situations demonstrate the importance of this balance...

Another example involves public access to government surveillance programs...

The European Union’s GDPR and similar privacy laws require governments to protect personal data carefully...

Cybersecurity protects the systems that allow society to function...

The world must move forward with a strong commitment to both values...

Ultimately, cybersecurity and FOI together help create a society that is both safe and free...
